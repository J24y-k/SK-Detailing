Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot. However, we'll do our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Do you provide your own water and electricity?

We bring our own supplies! We only need access to electricity for certain equipment and a tap to connect our hose pipe. If electricity isn't available, please let us know in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, & EFT. Payment is due upon completion of service.

What if I need to reschedule?

We understand plans change! Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule, and we'll find a new time that works for you.

How long will the service take?

Wash & Go takes approximately 45 minutes, while our Full Detail service takes around 4 hours. We'll provide a more accurate estimate based on your vehicle's condition.

Do I need to be present during the service?

No, you don't need to be present! Many of our clients continue with their work or daily activities. Just ensure we have access to your vehicle and a way to contact you when we're done.
